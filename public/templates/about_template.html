About

Crush routes is the wikipedia for climbing routes! Find your next big project, either being rock, boulder or ice. Climbers from all over the world can share their inspiring climbing routes with the community.

Route Information

Click on a marker to reveal individual route information.

Filter by Difficulty

Select minimum and maximum climb grades for your desired climb category.

Add Route

Login then click on the map to add your inspiring climbing route to the community.

Climbing Areas

Scroll to the bottom to view a list of climbing areas. Click on a desired climbing area to reveal a list of routes in that area.
